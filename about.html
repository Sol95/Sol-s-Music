ABOUT

Sol is a multi-instrumentalist and music lover. His music journey at the age of 12 when he started learning the drums. As a young teen he was inspired by various genres including pop-punk, rock, reggae, funk and much more. He played in several bands throughout high school and collaborated with other musicians to create original music Some major influential bands of this time included blink-182, the Red Hot Chilli Peppers, Rage Against The Machine as well as countless others.
